Given two strings S and T of length n and m respectively. 
Find count of distinct occurrences of T in S as a sub-sequence.

Sample TestCase:

Input:
S = "banana" , T = "ban"
Output:
3
Explanation:
[ban], [ba n], [b an].

Constraints:
1<=length of(T)<=100
1<=length of(S)<=8000
